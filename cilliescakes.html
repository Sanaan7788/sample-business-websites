Our Story

A Baker's Passion, Turned into Sweet Creations

Cilliescakes began as a heartfelt passion for baking and a desire to make every celebration unforgettable. Based in Houston, TX, we've been bringing custom cake dreams to life for families, couples, and businesses throughout the community.

We believe a cake is so much more than dessert — it's the centerpiece of your most cherished memories. That's why every order receives personalized attention from design to delivery, ensuring your cake is as delicious as it is beautiful.

From intricate wedding tiers to whimsical children's birthday cakes, our creative cake artists work with you every step of the way. We use only premium, fresh ingredients to make sure every bite is as memorable as the occasion itself.

How to Order

Your Custom Cake in 4 Easy Steps

Please allow a minimum of 2 weeks' notice for most orders, and 4–6 weeks for wedding cakes.

1

Get in Touch

Call, email, or fill out our quote form with your event date, occasion, and cake ideas.

2

Consultation & Design

We'll discuss your vision, flavors, size, and design details. Wedding orders include a tasting session.

3

Confirm & Deposit

Approve your design and place a deposit to secure your date. Your cake is then scheduled for baking.

4

Pickup or Delivery

Your freshly baked, beautifully decorated cake is ready for pickup or can be delivered to your venue.
